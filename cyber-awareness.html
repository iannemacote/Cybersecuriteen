Cyber Awareness

Cyber awareness means understanding online risks and adopting safe digital habits.

Common Cyber Awareness Techniques

  • Recognizing fake news and misinformation
  • Understanding social media privacy settings
  • Knowing how to respond to cyberbullying

How to Protect Yourself

  • Think before you share personal information online
  • Adjust privacy settings on social media
  • Educate yourself about emerging cyber threats
