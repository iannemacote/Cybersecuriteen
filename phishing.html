Phishing

Phishing is a cyber attack where scammers impersonate legitimate sources to steal personal information.

Common Phishing Techniques

  • Fake emails that look like they’re from banks or companies
  • Phony websites that steal login credentials
  • SMS phishing (smishing) that tricks users into clicking malicious links

How to Protect Yourself

  • Never click on suspicious links in emails or messages
  • Always verify the sender’s identity
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
