Scam Prevention

Scam prevention involves recognizing and avoiding fraudulent schemes that target individuals online.

Common Scam Prevention Techniques

  • Tech support scams pretending to fix non-existent problems
  • Romance scams that manipulate victims for money
  • Job scams that require payment upfront

How to Protect Yourself

  • Never share personal details with unverified sources
  • Do a background check before trusting online offers
  • Report suspicious activity to authorities
