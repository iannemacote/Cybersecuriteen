Online Fraud

Online fraud involves deceptive practices to scam people out of money or personal data.

Common Online Fraud Techniques

  • Fake online shopping sites that steal credit card info
  • Investment scams promising high returns
  • Lottery scams claiming you've won money

How to Protect Yourself

  • Always verify sellers before making online purchases
  • Be skeptical of deals that seem too good to be true
  • Never send money to strangers online
