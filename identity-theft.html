Identity Theft

Identity theft occurs when criminals steal your personal information for fraud or financial gain.

Common Identity Theft Techniques

  • Data breaches that expose private information
  • Social engineering to trick people into sharing sensitive details
  • Using stolen credit card numbers for purchases

How to Protect Yourself

  • Shred important documents before disposing of them
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Monitor bank and credit card statements regularly
